About Us

The Uganda Wildlife Conservation Society (UWCS) is dedicated to preserving Uganda's rich biodiversity and protecting endangered species. Our mission is to foster environmental sustainability through community-driven conservation programs and education.

Through partnerships, research, and outreach, we aim to create a harmonious balance between human activities and wildlife conservation in Uganda.

Our Conservation Projects

Mountain Gorilla Protection

This project works to protect the endangered mountain gorilla through habitat restoration, anti-poaching efforts, and local community education.
